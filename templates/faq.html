Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Solutions for Overcoming Community Hurdles Factory

To upload a Problem, go to the upload page, fill in the details, and choose a file or URL to upload.

Yes, you can upload multiple files at once on the upload page.

You can upload documents, images, videos, and links as Problems.

To delete a Problem, go to your Problems, find the one you want to delete, and click the delete button.

To edit a Problem, click on it in your Problems list and select the edit option.

You can share a Problem by clicking the share button and choosing the sharing method.

Yes, you can set your Problem to private in the privacy settings when uploading.

You can check your upload status in the uploads section of your profile.

Once you upload a Problem, it will be processed and made available in your account.

To register, go to the registration page, fill in your details, and submit the form. Make sure your email is your enrollment number.

To update your profile, go to your profile settings and edit the required fields.

Your profile contains your Problems, favorites, and personal information.

To change your password, go to your profile settings and select 'Change Password'.

To change your email address, go to your profile settings and update your email information.

You can view your activity log in the account settings under 'Activity'.

To add a bio, go to your profile settings and fill in the bio section.

To delete your account, please contact support for assistance.

Favorites are Problems that you have marked for easy access later.

You can view your favorites in the favorites section of your profile.

To add a Problem to favorites, click on the star icon next to the Problem.

To remove a Problem from favorites, click on the star icon again.

You can view recommendations based on your uploads and preferences in the recommendations section.

Recommendations are generated based on your activity and preferences.

You can contact support by filling out the contact form available on the contact page.

To report an issue, please fill out the report form in the help section.

Community guidelines outline the expected behavior and rules for using the platform. You can view them on the community guidelines page.

We value your feedback! You can provide feedback through the feedback form available in the help section.

To request a feature, please fill out the feature request form in the help section.

You can check the status of your support ticket in the support section of your account.
