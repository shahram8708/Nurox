Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us at Nurox. We ensure your data is protected and used transparently.

1. Information Collection

We collect the following information when you interact with our platform:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, and other details shared during registration.
  • Usage Data: Automatically collected data, including IP address, browser type, and access times for security and analysis purposes.
2. Use of Information

Your information is used for the following purposes:

  • Service Improvement: To enhance your experience and tailor our services to your needs.
  • Communication: To send important notifications and updates related to your account or our services.
  • Security: To maintain the safety of your account and our platform.
3. Data Protection & Security

We are committed to ensuring the security of your personal information. Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Access controls and security protocols to restrict unauthorized account access.
  • Regular audits and system checks to ensure data protection.
4. Sharing of Information

Your personal information will never be sold or shared with third parties without your consent, except in the following cases:

  • When required by law or to comply with legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Nurox and its users.
5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to improve your experience by customizing content and tracking usage patterns. You can manage your cookie preferences via your browser settings.

6. User Rights & Control

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information at any time. For any privacy concerns, or if you wish to exercise these rights, please reach out to us via our Contact Us page.

7. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Significant changes will be communicated to you through email or platform notifications. By continuing to use our services, you agree to the updated terms.

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, feel free to Contact Us. We are dedicated to ensuring your privacy and security.
